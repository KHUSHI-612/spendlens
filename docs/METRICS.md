# North Star Metrics - SpendLens

The success of SpendLens is measured by its ability to identify waste and capture intent for high-value procurement services (Credex).

## 1. North Star Metric: Total Potential Savings Surfaced (TPSS)
*   **Definition**: The aggregate sum of all `totalMonthlySavings` generated across all successful audits.
*   **Why**: This represents the core value proposition. If we surface $1M in savings, we have validated the problem exists at scale. It aligns user value (saving money) with business value (justifying a procurement service).

## 2. Primary KPI: High-Intent Lead Conversion Rate
*   **Definition**: The percentage of users who qualify for the "Enterprise Opportunity" ($500+/mo savings) and successfully submit the Lead Capture form.
*   **Target**: 15% of high-savings users.
*   **Why**: These are the leads that Credex can actually monetize. A high-volume lead is worth 100x more than a "Spending Well" lead.

## 3. Engagement Metric: Viral Share Coefficient
*   **Definition**: Average number of unique visitors coming from a shareable `audit/[id]` link per original audit.
*   **Why**: SpendLens is designed as a growth loop. A high coefficient means the tool is useful enough for founders to show their peers or investors.

## 4. Engineering Metric: Audit Completion Rate
*   **Definition**: Percentage of users who land on the page and reach the `Audit Results` state.
*   **Goal**: > 65%.
*   **Why**: Measures the friction of the `SpendForm`. Low completion means the form is too complex or slow.

## 5. Performance Metric: Lighthouse Performance Score (LCP)
*   **Definition**: Largest Contentful Paint under 1.2s.
*   **Why**: In a world of "AI tools," speed is synonymous with quality. If the audit engine feels instant (deterministic), it builds trust in the results.
