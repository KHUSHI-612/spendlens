A: Never. Your audit is processed locally and anonymously. We don't ask for your name or credit card, and we don't share your stack details with third parties.

**Q: What tools do you cover?**
A: We currently cover the most popular developer and founder AI tools, including Cursor, Windsurf, GitHub Copilot, ChatGPT (Plus/Team/API), Claude (Pro/Team/API), Perplexity, and more. We add new tools weekly.

**Q: How is this different from just checking pricing pages myself?**
